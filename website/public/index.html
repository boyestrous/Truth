Connects directly to the original source

One of the biggest problems with mis-information is that fact-checks are disconnected from the original information. This allows confirmation bias to heavily influence opinions and leads only those who want to disprove something to go searching for a fact-check

group

Two way communication

Fact-checks are traditionally done by reporters or professional fact-checkers. However, with heavy confirmation bias and distrust of the "mainstream media", fact-checks are often ignored by those who don't agree. By enabling two-way communication, those who disagree are given a platform to provide alternative information and strengthen the quality of the fact-check.

gavel

Fact-validated

Anyone can argue that something is wrong or right. But only those who can back it up with real evidence are helping to move the conversation towards the truth. Our algorithms are designed to promote the sharing of evidence over opinion.

Help Us Grow

We're looking for truth-minded individuals to help us build our database of fact-checks. With this information, we will continue to refine the types of falsehoods that permeate our sources of information in order to better and more easily identify ways that we are being mislead.
